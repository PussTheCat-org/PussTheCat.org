War doesn't excuse censorship

The first public political stance of PussTheCat.org and TheFrenchGhosty.

What:


Starting March 02, 2022, and following the Russian invasion of Ukraine, the EU and UK have banned the state-sponsored media RT and Sputnik over "disinformation".


The Problem:

This is censorship, and goes against the:

  • "Freedom of Speech": "Freedom of speech is a principle that supports the freedom of an individual or a community to articulate their opinions and ideas without fear of retaliation, censorship, or legal sanction."

  • "Freedom of Information": "Freedom of information is freedom of a person or people to publish and consume information."

    • and more importantly the:

  • "Freedom of the Press": "Freedom of the press or freedom of the media is the fundamental principle that communication and expression through various media, including printed and electronic media, especially published materials, should be considered a right to be exercised freely. Such freedom implies the absence of interference from an overreaching state; its preservation may be sought through constitution or other legal protection and security."

Our Stance:

War, even if you and I disagree with it, doesn't excuse ANY censorship from our countries in ANY way.
To be able to get critical of the information you read/see, you have to see both side of the conflict. This includes reading/seeing the propaganda of both side.
No censorship is ever acceptable, because censorship is a slippery slope.


The solution:

  • Use a VPN located outside of the countries that banned them. I recommend the most private one in my Privacy Guide.

  • For Twitter profiles, since PussTheCat.org is affected by this ban, our Nitter instance can't display the content. I personally recommend nitter.kavin.rocks (based in India).
PS: If you can, donate to the Ukraine Red Cross
