Privacy Policy

This page list what each public services and games hosted on PussTheCat.org collect:

Services:

Nginx:


Every service are hosted behind nginx, meaning every requests goes through it.

nginx log the following for every request:

  • Timestamp.
  • Method and URL.
  • User agent.
  • Anonymised IP:
    • IPv4: 116.202.209.166 stored as 0.202.209.166
    • IPv6: 2a01:4f8:241:16d9::2 stored as 0:4f8:241::

Invidious:

  • Not deployed yet.

Nitter:

  • Errors.
    • Example: WARN Incorrect query. Got: 12345678

Bibliogram:

  • Timestamp.
  • Type (API / INC / DIR).
  • Endpoint.

SearX:

  • Errors.

Retention period:

Every logs are stored less than a month. The date at which they were last deleted is February 20, 2020.



Sharing:

Nothing is never shared with anyone.


Access to your informations:

Since we don't collect or store "your" data and since the logs aren't associated with "you", we cannot export or delete "your" data.
